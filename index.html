Cancer Deaths by US County

The map was built using CartoDB and uses a custom CRS.

The data describe cancer rates for all cancer sites by mortality, for all races, sexes, and ages, averaged over the years of 2008 - 2012.

  • See the map: Cancer Deaths by US County
  • Data Source: National Cancer Institute’s State Cancer Profiles on 13 NOV 2015

US Fossil Fuel Power Plants

This map was built using Javascript and Leaflet.

It shows fossil fuel plants across the United States. By clicking on the map the user will see all indicated plants within a 500 km radius to the click point and the plant pop ups will be dsplayed when a user clicks on the plant circle. Circles indicate kilowatt output by the circle's size.

  • See the map: US Fossil Fuel Power Plants
  • Data Sources: US Energy Information Administration
